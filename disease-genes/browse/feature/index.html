Feature Info

The table shows the feature-specific performance for each disease. The number of associations for each disease is displayed in the associations column. The auroc — area under the ROC curve — indicates the ability of the feature to rank the associated genes above unassociated genes. The model_auroc column shows the disease-specific performance of the logistic ridge regression model fit on all gene-disease pairs. Clicking on a disease name loads the association predications for that disease. Clicking on a disease_code opens an external link with additional disease information. Our manual disease classifications are displayed in the pathophysiology column.
