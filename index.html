HETIONET
Explore Studies Software
Hetionet is an integrative network of biomedical knowledge assembled from 29 different public databases of genes, compounds, anatomy, diseases, symptoms, side effects, biological processes, cellular components, molecular functions, pathways, and pharmacologic classes. The database combines over 50 years of biomedical information into a single, convenient resource, consisting of 47,031 nodes (11 types) and 2,250,197 relationships (24 types).
